Crazy Eights

What suit would you like the 8 to be?

♣ ♦ ♥ ♠

The rules are easy!

  • The object of the game is to empty your hand first.
  • You can play an 8 at any time. If you play an 8, you pick what suit you want it to have.
  • You can play any card that either matches in suit or value to the card on the top of the pile.
  • If you don't have a card to play, select one from the deck!
